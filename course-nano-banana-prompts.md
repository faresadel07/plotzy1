# Nano Banana prompt pack for Plotzy course art (optional, bonus)

These are optional decorative illustrations. The course already ships with
real CC0 photographs and clean vector diagrams, so it stands on its own.
Use this pack only if you want richer, custom hero art. Generate each in
Google's Nano Banana (or any image model), then send me the files and I
will wire them in.

## How to use
1. Generate at 16:9 (landscape) unless noted. Ask for high resolution.
2. Keep a consistent look across the whole set: cinematic, warm, editorial,
   a little moody, shallow depth of field, natural light. NOT cartoonish,
   NOT glossy 3D render, NOT stock-clipart.
3. Do not put any text or letters inside the image (labels are added in code).
4. Send the files named exactly as the "save as" line says.

## Shared style suffix (paste at the end of every prompt)
"cinematic editorial photograph, warm natural light, shallow depth of field,
muted filmic color grade, fine grain, no text, no words, no watermark,
high detail, 16:9"
