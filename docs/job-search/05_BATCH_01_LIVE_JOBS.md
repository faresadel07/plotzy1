## What "Variant" means for the cover letter

Open `01_COVER_LETTERS.md`. Find the variant letter (A, B, or C)
listed for each job. That is the cover letter template to use. The
custom hook above goes in the {HOOK} placeholder of that variant.

So for job #1 Form AI (Variant A), open Variant A in
`01_COVER_LETTERS.md`, copy the whole thing, replace {COMPANY} with
"Form AI", {ROLE} with "Full-Stack AI Engineer", and {HOOK} with the
sentence I wrote for it above.

Total time per application: 3 to 5 minutes.
