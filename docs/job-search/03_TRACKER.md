# Application Tracker — Faris Hamdan

One row per application. Update every time you apply, every time you
hear back, every interview. Treat this as the single source of truth
for the job search.

Status legend:

- `🟢 applied` — submitted, waiting for response
- `🟡 contacted` — recruiter or hiring manager replied
- `🔵 screening` — assessment, take-home, or first call scheduled
- `🟣 interview` — onsite (remote) panel scheduled
- `🟠 offer` — offer on the table
- `🔴 rejected` — declined, archived
- `⚪ ghosted` — 14+ days no reply, treat as rejected

(The emojis here are tracker-only, never in user-facing copy.)

## How to fill in each column

- **Date**: `YYYY-MM-DD` so it sorts.
- **Company**: official name (not the careers page URL).
- **Role**: exactly as the posting calls it (`Software Engineer II`,
  not `Software Engineer`).
- **Source**: where you found the job (Wellfound, Remote OK, YC,
  direct, referral, cold DM).
- **Salary**: if posted, write `$4K/mo` or `$50K/yr`. If not, write
  `not posted`.
- **CL Variant**: which cover letter variant you sent (A, B, or C from
  `01_COVER_LETTERS.md`).
- **Status**: the emoji + word.
- **Next step**: concrete action with a date. If "wait for reply",
  write `Follow up YYYY-MM-DD` for 5 business days out.

---

## Tips

- **Apply within 24 hours of posting**. Jobs older than 14 days are
  often already filled with a frontrunner.
- **Personalise one sentence per company**. Recruiters smell mass
  copy-paste in under 3 seconds.
- **Track LinkedIn message threads** here too, with `Source: LinkedIn`.
- **Update status every Friday** so the weekly summary stays honest.

---

## When to fire-and-forget vs really invest

- **Tier 3 board jobs**: fire-and-forget. Three minutes max per app.
- **Tier 1 direct applications**: invest 15 to 20 minutes per app to
  read the company blog and write one specific paragraph.
- **YC startups**: invest 10 minutes per app, read the company page
  on YC and mention what you noticed.
- **Tier 2 agencies (Toptal, Turing, Arc)**: 30 to 60 minutes for the
  initial application + screening prep.

---

## When to follow up

| Days since application | Action |
|---|---|
| 5 business days | Send a short follow-up (template in `01_COVER_LETTERS.md`) |
| 10 business days | One more follow-up if the role is still posted |
| 14 business days, no reply | Mark as `⚪ ghosted`, move to Archive |

Do not chase further. Energy goes to the next 25 applications.
