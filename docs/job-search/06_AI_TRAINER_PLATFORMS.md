## TIER 1 — Apply today (best fit for your profile)

### 1. Mercor — The top choice
- **URL:** https://work.mercor.com/
- **Pay:** $20 to $200/hour (your range, $40 to $100/hr realistic)
- **Country eligibility:** Global, accepts Jordan.
- **Application process:**
  1. Go to https://work.mercor.com/
  2. Click "Apply" or "Find work"
  3. Upload `Faris_Hamdan_CV.pdf`
  4. Link your GitHub (github.com/faresadel07/plotzy1)
  5. Link your LinkedIn
  6. Complete the AI-led video interview (30 min, no live coding,
     they ask about your projects)
  7. They match you to projects within 1 to 14 days
- **What the work actually is:** RLHF (reading AI outputs and rating
  them), prompt engineering, agentic AI training, code review for AI
  models, domain expertise tasks.
- **Why this is your best fit:** Mercor specifically prioritises
  candidates with shipped AI products. Plotzy is exactly the kind of
  evidence they screen for. The video interview is conversational,
  not technical.
- **What to emphasise:** Plotzy's 10+ AI features, the 12 Anthropic
  certifications, your bilingual Arabic ability (huge plus, very
  short supply on Mercor).

### 2. Outlier (Scale AI) — Second priority
- **URL:** https://outlier.ai/
- **Pay:** $15 to $50/hour (Coding STEM tasks $25 to $50/hr).
- **Country eligibility:** 50 countries supported. Jordan is
  reportedly accepted (confirmed by reviews in 2026 from MENA
  applicants).
- **Application process:**
  1. Go to https://outlier.ai/
  2. Create profile (4 steps: profile, skills, ID verification,
     skill tests)
  3. Required: valid ID, mobile phone, CV, LinkedIn
  4. Take skill assessments (these are written tests, NOT live
     coding)
  5. They match you to projects after passing
- **Work:** Writing prompts, grading rubrics, rating answers.
  Coding STEM tasks pay best.
- **Strategy for you:** Tag yourself as "Coding" and "STEM"
  specialist. Pass those skill tests with Plotzy as your project
  proof. Skip the Generalist track (low pay).

### 3. Surge AI — Premium, harder to enter
- **URL:** https://www.surgehq.ai/
- **Pay:** $20 to $100+/hour
- **Country eligibility:** Global, accepts Jordan.
- **Application:** https://www.surgehq.ai/apply
- **Work:** RLHF for major AI labs (OpenAI, Anthropic, others).
  Quality bar is high.
- **Strategy:** Apply with Plotzy + AI certs. Surge is selective but
  pays well if accepted.

## What NOT to do

- **Do not pay anyone for "training" or "guaranteed access"** to
  these platforms. All legitimate platforms are free to sign up.
- **Do not use a VPN to fake your location.** Outlier and Mercor
  detect this and ban accounts.
- **Do not lie about your degree or experience** on the
  application. Plotzy is enough proof on its own. Honesty wins on
  these platforms because every task is graded.
- **Do not skip the AI cert mention.** 12 Anthropic certs is rare,
  use it as your edge.
